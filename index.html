Indian Christian Revival Mission

ICRM was founded in 1977 at Devashola, in the Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu, South India

It was started with the Vision - Revival in India & back to Jerusalem. It's mission were 5 fold:

  • Prayer Promotion for revival through Prayer for Nations
  • Evangelism to selected regions to reach specific people groups
  • Cross-cultural church planting
  • Making ministers & leaders through Operation Harvest Ministerial Training Centre (OHMTC)
  • Social, medical & Educational services through charity for urban & rural enchancement (CURE)

- Apostle R. John Elamgoven (Founder & President)
